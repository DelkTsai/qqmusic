##### api:

JSONP https://c.y.qq.com/splcloud/fcgi-bin/gethotkey.fcg

##### Params

|Param|Value|
|:----:|:----:|
|jsonpCallback|hotSearchKeysmod_top_search|
|g_tk|5381|
|loginUin|0|
|hostUin|0|
|inCharset|utf8|
|outCharset|utf-8|
|notice|0|
|platform|yqq|
|needNewCode|0|
